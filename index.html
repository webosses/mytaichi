taichi & qigong courses

The goal of Mtaichi Courses is to help people to master the art of Taichi, improve their health and spread this ancient art to people they love.

Taichi

Taichi Eighteen Form

a simplefied version of traditional Chen Style Taichi(Dajia), comprised of most of the typical Chen Style Taichi movements.

traditional taichi 74 form

it's a gentle traditional taichi form which originally functioned as an entrance for begniners to the Taichi martial arts world. In more modern times, it's more connected with health benefits. such as cultivating Qi(energy), body alignment, balance and awareness. This form takes about 15 to 30 mins.

qigong

14 Qi Building Exercise

Ancient original Qi Building Exercise, it has been scientifically proven to have multiple health benefits, including stablising blood glucose, lowering blood pressure, reversing arthritis, improving sleep quality, etc.

Yijinjin(Shaolin Qigong)

Originally from the Shaolin Temple, the holy land of martial arts.

It is somewhat similar to Yoga, but much gentler, a good stretching exercise as well as Qi(energy) building practice.

About Me

I am a 12th. generation inheritor of Chen style Taijiquan Small Frame and a 13th. generation inheritor of Chen style Taijiquan Large Frame .

I am currently a sixth level Tai Chi practitioner, as certified by Hunan Province Wushu Association in 2017. I am also a certified Tai Chi teacher.

Awards

I have participated in many local, provincial, national and international martial arts competitions in such cities as Hong Kong, Changsha, Zhuzhou, Xiamen, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wenxian (where Chen Style Tai Chi originated) and many other places.
